This week was an introduction to English Literature on the Access to HE course.

We got to know each other a little bit.  Then you were given thisYear Planner with assignment dates for the year.

Throughout the year, we will be studying five different assignments:

 

•Short Fiction: I’ll give you the two short stories.
•Interpreting Poetry: I’ll give you the two poems.
•A Shakespeare Play: You’ll need a copy of The Merchant of Venice.
•Studying a Play: You’ll need a copy of Kindertransport by Diane Samuels.
•Studying a Novel: You’ll need a copy of The Color Purple by Alice Walker.


We looked at some definitions of literature and you decided which ones you liked or didn't like.  You also came up with some of your own definitions.

It will be important to use literary terms in your written work and discussions; we matched up a variety of terms with their correct definitions - see the list here.

In groups, you completed this literary quiz and we checked the answers.
