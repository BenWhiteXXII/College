This is the PowerPoint from this week's session.

We recalled some of last week's learning and you checked your homework for SPaG (spelling, punctuation and grammar) and submitted it for marking.  You'll get this back next week.

We went through the historical situation for women in 1893 when this story was published - all details are on the PowerPoint above and discussed how far the character of Lady Sannox fitted into the idealistic "angel of the house" of Victorian middle-class and upper-class womanhood.

We looked at using PEE (point; evidence; evaluation) paragraphs in your work to ensure you reach level 3 requirements.

These are examples of good, not bad and poor responses.
